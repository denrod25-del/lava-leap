Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This policy explains what data Lava Leap ("we", "us") collects, why, and what choices you have. Lava Leap is a browser and Android arcade game published by B. Symbolic. We keep data collection minimal — you can play without an account and without giving us anything.

The short version: Playing the game requires no account and no personal data. The only personal data we store is an email address — and only if you choose to join the waitlist. We don't sell your data. We don't run ads.

What we collect

Waitlist email (optional). If you submit your email to the waitlist, we store that email address and the date you joined. That's it — no name, no password, no profile.

Anonymous usage analytics. We use Vercel Web Analytics to understand how many people visit and which buttons get used. It is privacy-friendly by design: it does not use cookies, does not collect personally identifiable information, and does not track you across other websites. We may record aggregated, anonymous events such as a page view, a "play" click, or a waitlist signup — never tied to your identity.

Server logs. Our host (Vercel) and our database provider (Supabase) automatically process technical data such as IP address and browser type to serve the page and protect against abuse. This is standard for any website and is handled under their infrastructure.

What we do NOT collect

  • No accounts, usernames, or passwords for playing.
  • No advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.
  • No sale or sharing of your data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • No location, contacts, or device data beyond what a normal web page receives.

How we use your email

If you join the waitlist, we use your email only to send occasional updates about Lava Leap — new features, the Android release, and leaderboard seasons. We will not send you unrelated marketing or pass your address to third parties.

Where your data lives

Waitlist emails are stored in a Supabase (PostgreSQL) database protected by row-level security, so the list cannot be read from the public website. Analytics are processed by Vercel. Both are reputable infrastructure providers with their own security and privacy programs.

The Android app

The Android APK is distributed through GitHub Releases. Download it only from that official page — avoid third-party mirrors, which we cannot vouch for. The app itself does not require an account.

Your choices & rights

  • Unsubscribe from waitlist emails at any time using the link in any message we send.
  • Deletion: ask us to remove your email from the waitlist and we will delete it.
  • Access: ask what we hold about you (for the waitlist, it's just your email and join date).

To exercise any of these, email hello@bsymbolic.com.

Children

Lava Leap is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted an email, contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle data, we'll update this page and revise the "last updated" date above. Material changes affecting waitlist subscribers may also be sent by email.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@bsymbolic.com.
