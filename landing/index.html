MOVE

Run left and right across the tower. Down to fast-fall when you need to drop quick.

SPACE

JUMP + AGAIN

Space to jump — press again in the air for a double jump to reach the next ledge.

SHIFT

On mobile: hold the left half of the screen to run, tap the right half to jump — tap again mid-air for double and triple jumps.
